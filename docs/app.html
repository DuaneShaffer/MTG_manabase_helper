Your deck — castability on curve

Each card carries two readings. The badge (bottom-right, a letter + %) is the model grade: the chance the right colors are available by the turn you'd cast the card, assuming you keep an opener and hit your land drops (Frank Karsten's model) — so it tops out at 99%, never a guaranteed 100%, and climbs as you add lands. It does not model raw mana screw or flood. Press Simulate and a second pill (bottom-left) appears: the sim grade plays out thousands of real games and reports true on-curve castability, screw included. The land-count target is your safeguard against flood.
