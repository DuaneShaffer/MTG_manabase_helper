Your deck — castability on curve

Each card carries two readings, both updating live as you edit the build. The lower pill (marked with a mana-pie) is the colors reading — Frank Karsten's model: the chance the right colors are available by the turn you'd cast the card assuming you hit your land drops. It tops out at 99% and isolates color quality from everything else. The pill above it (marked with a clock) is the lands-on-time reading — it plays out thousands of real games (shuffle, mulligan, draw) and reports true on-curve castability including mana screw and flood. Read them together: if the mana-pie is high but the clock is low, your colors are fine and you're screwing or flooding — adjust the land count, not the fixing.
